🚀 Getting Started

Everything you need to begin your adventure in Camp of Legends.

🎮 What is Camp of Legends?

Camp of Legends is a Telegram RPG bot where you recruit heroes, send them on missions, collect loot, craft powerful gear, and build the strongest camp. The game runs passively — your heroes go out while you're away and bring back gold and items.

HOW TO START

  1. Open the bot on Telegram
    Visit t.me/TU_BOT and press Start. Your account is created automatically.
  2. Check your recruits
    Send recruits to see available heroes. You start with one free recruit slot.
  3. Recruit your first hero
    Send recruit [name] to accept a hero from the pool. Heroes have different classes — choose wisely!
  4. Send them on a mission
    Send send [hero] on [mission_id]. Try forest_patrol first — low difficulty, quick rewards.
  5. Check back later
    Send status any time — completed missions are automatically resolved and loot is added to your inventory.
  6. Equip, craft, grow
    Use equip [item] on [hero], craft recipes with craft [recipe], and unlock harder missions as your heroes level up.

💡 Tips for new players

  • Mission success depends on your hero's Power rating vs the mission's danger level.
  • Failed missions give 25% XP and may wound your hero — they'll recover after a short cooldown.
  • Equipping gear directly improves Power and your success chances on harder missions.
  • New hero slots unlock as your overall progress grows — complete missions to earn them.
  • Endgame gear (Shadow Blade, Dragonscale Vest, Champion's Sword) cannot be bought — only looted or crafted.

🗺️ Missions

Click any mission row to reveal its loot table. Mission success depends on your hero's Power vs the mission's danger rating.

⚡ Success Probability

Your hero's Power rating vs the mission's danger determines your odds. At equal power to danger you have ~50% success. Overpowered heroes approach 95% (cap). Failing a mission gives 25% consolation XP and may wound your hero temporarily.

🎒 Items

Complete item catalog. Weapons, armor, and accessories boost hero Power. Materials are used in crafting.

🔨 Crafting

Combine materials looted from missions into powerful gear. Use craft [recipe] in the bot once you have the required items.

🔑 Crafting Tips

  • Materials are dropped by missions — farm Ore Vein for Iron Ore, Shadow Throne for Dragon Scales.
  • Shadow Essence and Dragon Scales are the rarest materials — required for endgame crafting.
  • You can also buy some gear from the NPC Shop to skip early farming.

🛒 Shop

The NPC shop sells starter and mid-tier gear for gold. Endgame items must be looted or crafted. Use buy [item] to purchase and sell [item] to sell.

💰 Sell Prices

All items have a sell price regardless of whether they appear in the shop. Selling materials you don't need is a good source of income early on. Check the Items section for each item's sell value.
